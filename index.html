bio

an HCI/CSCW researcher; empathetic moderator; contemplative flaneur; resource-sharing strategist; lifetime learner (plus an EV enthusiast and an unapologetic YouTube binge-watcher).

Interested areas include account sharing, informal coordination, human agency, psychological ownership, the subscription economy, shared accountability, Human-AI communication, and Human-centered AI.
